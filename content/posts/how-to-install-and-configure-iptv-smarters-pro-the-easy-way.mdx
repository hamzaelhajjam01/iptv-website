IPTV Smarters Pro is one of the most widely-used and reliable IPTV player apps in the world. While it may not have the flashy design of TiviMate, it's famous for being stable, easy to use, and compatible with almost any IPTV service.

This guide will show you how to install and, most importantly, configure the app using your provider's credentials.

## What is IPTV Smarters Pro? {#what-is-iptv-smarters-pro}

IPTV Smarters Pro is a free IPTV player. It does not provide any channels, movies, or content. It is an empty "player" that you must "load" by logging in with a subscription from an IPTV provider. It is the "engine," but you still need the "fuel."

## How to Install IPTV Smarters Pro {#how-to-install}

### On Android (Google Play Store) {#install-android}
If you are using an Android TV Box, smartphone, or tablet:
1.  Open the **Google Play Store**.
2.  Search for **"IPTV Smarters Pro"**.
3.  Click **"Install"**.

### On Amazon Firestick (Sideloading) {#install-firestick}
The app is not on the Amazon Appstore. You must sideload it.
1.  Enable "Apps from Unknown Sources" in your Firestick settings.
2.  Install the **"Downloader"** app.
3.  Open Downloader and enter the official developer shortcut code: **650963** (This will download the "Lite" version, which is recommended).
4.  Click "Go" to download, and then **"Install"**.

## How to Configure IPTV Smarters Pro (Easy Login) {#how-to-configure}

This is the most important part. When you open the app, it will give you several options.

### Step 1: Open the App and Accept Terms {#step-1-open-app}

Open IPTV Smarters Pro. You will be asked to accept their terms of use. Click **"Accept"**.

### Step 2: Choose 'Login with Xtream Codes API' {#step-2-login-method}

The app will offer multiple ways to log in, such as "Load Your Playlist (M3U)" or "Login with Xtream Codes API."

Always choose **"Login with Xtream Codes API"** (it may be called "Login with Username and Password"). This is the easiest and most reliable method.

### Step 3: Enter Your Credentials {#step-3-enter-details}

Your IPTV provider (like us) will send you three pieces of information in your welcome email.
On this screen, you must fill in all four boxes:

1.  **Any Name:** This is just a label. You can type anything, like "StreamVerse" or "My TV."
2.  **Username:** (Enter your provider's username exactly)
3.  **Password:** (Enter your provider's password exactly)
4.  **Server URL:** (Enter your provider's Server URL, e.g., `http://example.com:1234`)

After filling in all four fields, click **"Add User"**. The app will take a moment to "Download channels, movies, and EPG." Once it's done, you're ready to stream.

## Common Issues & Troubleshooting {#common-issues}

* **Error: "Invalid Username, Password, or URL"**: 99% of the time, this means you have a typo. Double-check your details, make sure you respect case-sensitivity (e.g., 'User' is different from 'user'), and ensure there are no extra spaces.
* **Error: "Network Connection Failed"**: This means your internet is down or the Server URL is incorrect.

If you continue to have problems, our support team is available 24/7. Visit our [Help Center](/help) for assistance.
