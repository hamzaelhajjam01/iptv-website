While a Firestick is simple, a dedicated Android TV box often offers more power, flexibility, and app access. But the market is flooded with devices, from $20 budget boxes to $200 powerhouses.

We've ranked the best options for any budget, focusing on performance and value for IPTV streaming.

## Premium vs. Budget: What's the Difference? {#premium-vs-budget-difference}

* **Premium Boxes ($150+):** These devices, like the Nvidia Shield, have powerful processors, more RAM, and advanced features like AI 4K upscaling. They are ideal for power users, gamers, and those who demand flawless 4K 60fps performance.
* **Budget Boxes (<$60):** These devices are built for streaming and do it well. They are perfect for most IPTV users who just want to stream HD or 4K content without lag.

## The Best Premium Android TV Boxes {#best-premium-android-tv-boxes}

### 1. Nvidia Shield TV Pro (Best Overall) {#premium-nvidia-shield-pro}

The Nvidia Shield TV Pro is, without question, the "king" of all streaming devices. It is a high-performance mini-computer. Its powerful processor can handle any video format you throw at it, and its "AI-Enhanced Upscaling" makes HD content look shaper on 4K TVs. It's also a full-fledged gaming console.
* **Best For:** Power users, gamers, and those who want the absolute best, fastest performance with zero compromises.
* **Downside:** Very expensive (around $200).

### 2. Amazon Fire TV Cube (Best for Alexa Users) {#premium-fire-tv-cube}

If you're in the Amazon ecosystem, the Fire TV Cube is the top-of-the-line option. It combines a powerful 4K Fire TV streamer with a built-in Alexa speaker for hands-free voice control. It's fast, supports Wi-Fi 6E, and is a great choice for a high-end smart home.
* **Best For:** Users who want a premium 4K streaming experience and are already invested in Amazon Alexa.
* **Downside:** Still uses the "closed" FireOS, requiring sideloading for many IPTV apps.

## The Best Budget Android TV Boxes {#best-budget-android-tv-boxes}

### 1. Onn. Google TV 4K Pro Box (Best Value) {#budget-onn-4k-pro}

This Walmart-exclusive box is the hidden gem of the streaming world. For a very low price (often around $50), you get a fully-certified Google TV device that is fast, supports 4K, and has a great remote. It's widely considered the best value-for-money streamer on the market.
* **Best For:** Everyone on a budget. It's the best cheap Android TV box, period.
* **Downside:** Only sold at Walmart in the US.

### 2. Xiaomi TV Box S (2nd Gen) (Best International Budget) {#budget-xiaomi-tv-box-s}

Xiaomi has a long reputation for making high-quality, affordable electronics. The TV Box S is a sleek, powerful, and affordable 4K streamer that runs the official Google TV operating system. It's a fantastic, reliable choice that is available worldwide.
* **Best For:** Budget-conscious users outside the US who want a reliable, Google-certified device.
* **Downside:** Not as powerful as the premium options, but more than enough for IPTV.

### 3. Chromecast with Google TV (4K) (Best for Simplicity) {#budget-chromecast-google-tv}

This device from Google is the gold standard for a simple, reliable streaming experience. It runs the clean, easy-to-use Google TV interface, supports 4K HDR, and has a simple remote with Google Assistant.
* **Best For:** Users who want a simple, no-fuss "it just works" experience from Google.
* **Downside:** It's a "dongle," so it has no extra ports (like USB or Ethernet).
